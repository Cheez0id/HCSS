We Invent, design, patent, and manufacture customized storage innovations for tools, ammunition, and firearms.

Hardcore Storage Solutions, LLC. is an innovative American-owned-and-operated small-business that specializes in inventing, designing, patenting, and manufacturing customized storage solutions for tools, ammunition, and firearms. Our flagship product, CanMod, is a patent-pending, modular storage device that fits perfectly into a 50 caliber or 30 caliber Army ammunition can. Unlike other products in the market, CanMod allows customers to fully utilize the space in a your ammo can by offering customizable trays that can be tailored to hold various items such as ammo, batteries, tools, cleaning equipment, and more.

We are an engaged development-team based out of Georgia; By leveraging our in-house manufacturing capabilities, we can ensure high-quality production and offer made-to-order trays that meet the specific needs of our customers. With our unique solutioning, design expertise, and utilization of 3D printing (Additive Manufacturing), we aim to provide excellent products that just make sense.

Our product is designed, developed, and manufactured in the USA, and we strive to source materials produced in the USA for printing. Read more about Berry Compliance here.
